I'm Thobani Maluleka, a Candidate Land Surveyor

Thobani Maluleka is a highly motivated Student Geomatician (Specialising in Land Surveying and GIS) at the University of Cape Town, with experience in project planning, management and leadership skills. Thobani believes mindfulness in the workplace is key to success and can be reached via email.
